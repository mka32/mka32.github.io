Overview of India's Foreign Policy

India's foreign policy is shaped by its values of non-alignment, regional cooperation, and active global engagement. As a key player on the world stage, India maintains strategic relationships with countries across Asia, Africa, Europe, the Americas, and the Indo-Pacific region.
